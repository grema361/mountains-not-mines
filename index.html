Aotearoa New Zealand • Ki uta ki tai

Protect mountains.
Reject destructive mining.

Mountains Not Mines exists to protect the mauri and integrity of Aotearoa New Zealand’s hills, uplands and mountains, their indigenous biodiversity, ecology and scenic values — and to promote ki uta ki tai stewardship from mountains to the sea.

What we do

We defend mountain ecosystems and the places downstream that depend on them. We oppose destructive mining and inappropriate development, and promote sustainable land and water stewardship.

Protect

Defend high-value mountainscapes, waterways and habitats from industrial mining and inappropriate development.

Advocate

Champion strong protections for biodiversity, water quality and landscape integrity — grounded in evidence.

Connect

Promote ki uta ki tai: what happens in headwaters can shape ecosystems and water quality at the coast.
